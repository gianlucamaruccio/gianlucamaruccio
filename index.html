cars don't cry

2023, performance, 6’, in collaboration with Vivi Jordi, for Dusty Dog Festival, Flawil

I dress in a Mercedes Benz costume and let myself be washed in a car wash. The wash cycle consists of the following steps:

    Step 1: Powerschaum

    Step 2: Waschen

    Step 3: Schaumbürste

    Step 4: Klarspülen

    Step 5: Wachs

    Step 6: Glanzspülen

Every step was accompanied by its own song. You can listen to the soundtrack here:

Cars Don’t Cry Soundtrack on Spotify
